IT Village

Artificial Intelligence : what are the business applications ?

It is for this reason that IT VILLAGE undertakes to connect not only the professionals of the sector or the winners of the Big Engineering Schools of Morocco, but also the students and future engineers in a friendly space that will allow them, among other things, to :

  • Find out new developments in the IT field.
  • Debate, exchange, share and communicate on the subject of IT products.
  • Popularize new IT solutions through targeted training
  • Create professional links between the alumni and the students in a "Second chance of employment" approach
  • Present the services, products and innovative solutions of the IT field

Our event is an exceptional day around a program that combines exhibitions, exchanges, trainings as well as a competition. IT Village is an annual event that brings together the key players in the IT field at the national level organized by the INSEA IT club in collaboration with SIGMA 21, it being located in the National Institute of Statistics and Applied economics on december 14-15, under the theme of IT VILLAGE: Artificial Intelligence : what are the business applications ?

  • Date: december, 14th and 15th 2018
  • Place: INSEA, Rabat
