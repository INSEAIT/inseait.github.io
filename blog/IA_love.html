How I felt in Love with Geoffery Hinton

Let’s together fight for humanity using AI

Posted by Sara Hamida on March 06, 2019

          For those of you who doesn’t know who Geoffry Hinton is I invite you to make a simple research on google, it’ll be worth the effort. for those who knew the man I congratulate you you’re a passionate IA person and you’d probably understand the title more quickly, it’s simply the love of AI field and what good it can do for the world, now let me tell you how this love started.

          From an early age, and like any other child I was always asked that classical question of : what do you want to be in the future, my answer was always : a Doctor, why?
because I used to watch a Korean drama about doctors, hospitals, operation rooms and saving people’s lives, The reason may be too childish for some of you, but let’s be tolerant considering my age back then.



But I’ll admit I still love watching shows about doctors and their volunteering work, the lives they have the chance to daily save, and I envy them for the opportunity they were given to do such a human work, the opportunity I couldn’t and I could never have as an engineer, Or so I used to think, until I discovered the world of AI, the world built by people like Geoffry Hinton, Andrew Ng and their gang. I finally found the place where I can be a doctor, a savior without putting the white apron nor the stethoscope, a world where you can hold more power in your pocket than your entire home, a world where you can literally change the world.

Let’s get some motivation

          The good news is, we’re very lucky to live now to witness the IA revolution and to be part of it, it’s an era where we have all the resources we need to be part of this revolution, to get into the boat with these great people who wants to use IA for humanitarian purposes, everything is available now to do so, take the huge community Git built by this interesting human being Linus Torvalds who had this vision of creating a community where everyone can contribute to change the world see his Ted Talk here, See also the article about Git of my friend Nacer Sebti And you have to definitely check the project made by my dear friends Zack and Ihab helping people who are visually impaired to detect obstacles in their way and notify them with their phones, and of course it’s open source, you can check on Github

ahead and start building your own AI app, and leave your mark in this world before leaving it.
           I’ll make this short and I will leave you with a video which perhaps can change something in you, it’s a call from Microsoft to all the fans of Geoffrey Hinton to come abroad the ship of fighting against childhood Malnutrition, have fun and don’t ever forget that a small group of thoughtful people can change the world, it’s the only thing that ever has.
