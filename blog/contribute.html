How to contribute

INSEA IT article guidelines
  1. Article length :
  • In general no restrictions.
  • The article should be a minimum of 1500 words up to 3500 words (the standard article length).
  • For specific types of articles (reviews, commentary...) please do consult the editorial team.
  • No decisions made without consulting the editorial team!
  1. Article structure:
  • In general no restrictions.
  • Standard structure like: tutorials, how-to articles, book reviews, commentaries...
  • If in doubt, please do consult our editorial team.
  1. Article formatting:
  • Any editable format is acceptable: .doc, .docx, etc-No pdf formats!
  • No restrictions on font pages or margins.
  • Images are preferred to be sent separately in jpg or png format.
  • Every picture sent should be labelled.
  • Videos included should be accompanied with their link.
  1. Article submission process:
  • The topic would be set by the editorial team.
  • The timeframe would be discussed.
  • Article gets written and set to our team.
  • Grammar and other revisions will be made.
  • The article is published.

 

 

 

 

The following format must be respected and for any further please do contact our team:

  • Introduction and conclusion:
     every article should start and end with a conclusion. No more than 6-8 sentences max where you introduce your topic; same thing applies to the conclusion.
  • Paragraphs:
     feel free to construct your paragraphs as you wish, if necessary the paragraphs should have headings.
  • Graphics:
     you can insert pictures or screenshots in your article, especially when it comes to a tutorial or how-to format. All the pictures should labelled
  • If the article includes a video it should be linked in the “web and bios” section at the end of the article.
  • All biographies and references should linked in the “web and bios” section.
  • If the author likes to add links for further reading, they can be added in the “web and bios” section too.
  • About the author :
    it’s the last section of the article where a photo of the author is disposed along with a bio written by the author himself.

Example

 

TITLE

By your name

 

Introduction:

            This is where you get to introduce you topic to the readers, make it short concise and straight-forward. If not you can always set your goals or what the reader will learn throughout the article.

Paragraphs:

            This is the main part of the article, so you get to do as you please with it. Don’t forget to add pictures and graphics especially when it comes to tutorials.

Conclusion:

            A small summary of what had been said or maybe results.

Web and bios:

            All of your references should be named in this section, whether they are books or internet references.

Other links or articles discussing such topic you might add here as well.

About the author:

            Include your picture along with anything you would like to share about yourself.
